# REP-03 · Portfolio Health Aggregation API

**Timestamp**: 22:15:00 Local Time  
**Author**: Ralph  

## Why
Portfolio managers need a high-level view of health across all active projects. This endpoint aggregates key metrics like overdue tasks, health scores, and risk levels to enable proactive management of the entire project portfolio.

## What Changed
- `backend/app/schemas/portfolio.py`: Created schemas for portfolio health data.
- `backend/app/api/api_v1/endpoints/projects.py`:
    - Implemented `/portfolio/health` endpoint.
    - Added logic to calculate health scores based on overdue tasks and project status.
    - Added risk assessment logic (Low/Medium/High).
    - Aggregated portfolio-wide stats like average progress and critical project count.
- `backend/test_portfolio_logic.py`: Verified the health aggregation logic with a unit test.

## Notes
- The health score is currently a weighted deduction from 100 based on overdue tasks.
- Critical projects are flagged if their risk level is "High".
- Superusers can view health for all projects; standard users are limited to projects they own.
