# REAL-02 · Deprecate legacy Idea Comments backend

**Timestamp**: 14:35:00 Local Time  
**Author**: Ralph  

## Why
After migrating the frontend to the universal threaded commenting system, the legacy `idea_comments` backend logic (model, schema, CRUD, and API) became redundant and was removed to maintain a clean codebase.

## What Changed
- Deleted `backend/app/models/idea_comment.py`.
- Deleted `backend/app/schemas/idea_comment.py`.
- Deleted `backend/app/crud/crud_idea_comment.py`.
- Deleted `backend/app/api/api_v1/endpoints/idea_comments.py`.
- `backend/app/models/__init__.py`: Removed legacy import.
- `backend/app/api/api_v1/api.py`: Unregistered the `/ideas/{idea_id}/comments` routes.
- `backend/app/models/idea.py`: Removed the legacy `comments` relationship.
- `backend/app/schemas/idea.py`: Switched from legacy comments to `threaded_comments`.
- `backend/app/crud/crud_idea.py`: Updated eager loading to use the universal `threaded_comments` relationship.
- `backend/reset_db_internal.py`: Removed cleanup logic for legacy idea comments.
