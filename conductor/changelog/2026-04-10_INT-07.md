**Timestamp**: 20:45:00 Local Time  
**Author**: Ralph  

## Why
Visualizing the team's workload is critical for project management. A heatmap widget on the dashboard provides an immediate overview of team capacity, highlighting bottlenecks and overallocated individuals.

## What Changed
- `frontend/src/pages/dashboard.tsx`:
    - Integrated the `/api/v1/dashboard/team-workload` endpoint.
    - Implemented a 14-day horizontal heatmap table.
    - Added color-coded cells based on daily effort: Low (Emerald), Medium (Blue), Full (Amber), and Overallocated (Rose + Pulse animation).
    - Added sticky headers and columns for better usability on wide tables.
    - Integrated a legend for the workload categories.

## Notes
- The heatmap uses a 14-day window by default but is designed to be easily adjustable.
- It provides a granular view of both daily hours and task counts per user.
- Overallocated users (> 8h/day) are highlighted with a pulsing red effect to draw immediate attention.
