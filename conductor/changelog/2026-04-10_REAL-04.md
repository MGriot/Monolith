# REAL-04 · Broadcast Notifications via WebSockets

**Timestamp**: 15:10:00 Local Time  
**Author**: Ralph  

## Why
Users need real-time feedback when they receive new assignments or when tasks they are waiting for are unblocked. WebSockets provide a low-latency way to push these updates to the client without polling.

## What Changed
- `backend/app/crud/crud_notification.py`: Overrode the `create` method to automatically push new notifications to the user's active WebSocket connections via the `ConnectionManager`.
- `backend/app/api/api_v1/endpoints/notifications.py`: Added a new WebSocket endpoint `/ws/{token}` that validates the user's JWT and manages the connection lifecycle.
- `backend/test_ws_broadcast.py`: Created a standalone test script to verify that the broadcast logic is triggered during notification creation.

## Notes
